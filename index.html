1. G.R.A.V.I.T.Y.

For example, creatures living thousands of meters below the ocean can withstand tremendous pressures, but die when they rise to the water surface. The same is true for us. Mankind begins to exhibit anatomical changes in a spaceship with almost zero gravity on long spacewalks. Bones weaken or the digestive system is disturbed. And despite these, if they live in the same conditions for hundreds of years, either anatomy keeps up with this or the continuation of its species disappears day by day. So, what dangers could be waiting for people in a world where gravity is decreasing day by day? Could people who have been able to find a solution to just about anything for centuries have been able to overcome this problem? Or would this problem be the end of the people who destroyed the world?
People ... It was they who will open the door to all the disasters we have experienced and will experience in the near future. And it seemed that they were faced with a huge problem that would profoundly affect humanity and the whole living world. But maybe they might have a chance. There were a number of young people who managed to remain different from everything and everyone in a world where technology progressed and people did not fit the definition of 'human'. And these young people were willing to sacrifice everything to give life to the planet they lived in.
***
Grace and Richard… They were two people who were devoted to all humanity. They were among those who realized that the world was waiting for a great catastrophe, and this situation set them in motion. This duo, who spent time together for years, came together with people who had similar ideas with them in the following years. They all gathered together for a common purpose. They aimed to save humanity and establish a brand new world order. Because that society they were in made it compulsory for them to do this. Grace and Richard had done some work to stop the impending disaster. But because they needed more people, they included 4 more people in their group. And he started transferring his plans to other members of the group. Grace first announced that she wanted to design a robot to make her work easier. But he said he needed a few people for design. Adrian and Victoria were involved in the speech and stated that they could help with the design and equipment of the robot. Tom said it would be a good idea to start a company by participating in the conversation. Everyone approved of Tom's opinion. After hours of succession, the meeting was over and many decisions had been made.
***

There were two people responsible for this company: Tom and Iris. The rest of the group began to follow the gravity decreasing day by day, and as a result of research, they could predict that if they did not discover a substitute for gravity, all of humanity would be destroyed. Everyone was aware that nothing could ever be the same. Humankind, who has slaughtered and concreted nature for years and caused various pollution with the things they consume or use, would pay a great price for this damage. But Richard had an idea as usual. And now he had to share this idea he was thinking about with others. Richard told his friends: Gravity is essential for living life to survive, and otherwise imagine that there is no gravity. This situation will turn a good day into a disaster. Many things like cars, people furniture, pens and papers on the table need gravity to stay on the floor. Everything that is not tied to the ground has no reason to stay on the ground and begins to drift. Without gravity, not only vehicles such as furniture and cars will be dragged, but also the atmosphere, which is very important for our lives, and the waters in oceans, lakes and rivers will also be dragged in the air. In short, if we do not find a solution to the gravity that is decreasing day by day, nobody will survive. I have an idea that I can propose against this situation and that we will develop by thinking on it together. As a result of my research, it is possible to produce an artificial gravity in space. Of course, although the possibility of realization is high, it is only a possibility as a result. But friends, when we think of the dwindling time, maybe this may be our last chance to save humanity. We must start working so that it is not late for everything. First of all, I want Tom and Iris to stay in the world. Because the company, and in some cases, will need them too much. Me and the rest behindWe will go to the space station we built last year with the necessary equipment and we will start working to find an artificial gravity. I know we don't have much time. In other words, time will advance rapidly on earth while we are in space. That's why we have to hurry up guys.
***
Richard's speech was effective for everyone and the necessary preparations were made. The necessary equipment was provided by the company.
